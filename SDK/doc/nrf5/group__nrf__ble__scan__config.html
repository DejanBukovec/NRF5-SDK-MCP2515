#define NRF_BLE_SCAN_SCAN_PHY

PHY to scan on.

  • 0 - BLE_GAP_PHY_AUTO

  • 2 - BLE_GAP_PHY_2MBPS
  • 4 - BLE_GAP_PHY_CODED
  • 255 - BLE_GAP_PHY_NOT_SET

Priorities 0,2 (nRF51) and 0,1,4,5 (nRF52) are reserved for SoftDevice

Following options are available:

  • 0 - 0 (highest)
  • 1 - 1
  • 2 - 2
  • 3 - 3
  • 4 - 4 (Applicable devices only)

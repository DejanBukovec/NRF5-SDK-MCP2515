#define APP_SDCARD_FREQ_DATA

SPI frequency.

Following options are available:

  • 33554432 - 125 kHz
  • 67108864 - 250 kHz
  • 134217728 - 500 kHz
  • 268435456 - 1 MHz
  • 536870912 - 2 MHz
  • 1073741824 - 4 MHz
  • 2147483648 - 8 MHz
